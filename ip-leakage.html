User IP Address Leakage via Unproxied External Resources

Date: November 20, 2025

Summary

During my recent analysis of [Target Name], I discovered that the application loads external resources (such as images or fonts) directly from third-party servers without proxying the request through the application's backend.

The Vulnerability

When a user visits the affected page, their browser automatically makes a request to the external URL. This reveals the user's Real IP address and User-Agent to the owner of that external resource.

Proof of Concept

I injected the following HTML into the profile description:

<img src="http://attacker-controlled-server.com/pixel.png" />

Impact

This allows an attacker to de-anonymize users, potentially correlating their activity on the platform with their physical location or identity.
